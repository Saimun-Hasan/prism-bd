About
Prism Venture

Prism Venture is a customer powered company with a vision of
“Total Customer Solution.”

We offer quality readymade garments at the right price. We source various types of shell fabric to a tag pin from different countries i.e. China, Taiwan, Thailand, Malaysia, India as per customer requirement. We are supported by our strong sampling / sourcing / merchandising / quality technician & shipping team to execute orders of all sizes.

Our goal is to provide a risk-free solution for sourcing fashion garments from Bangladesh and to ensure a secured business for our customers.
