About Prism

Welcome to Prism Venture – a buying house with a vision that aims to change the perspective of your garment sourcing experience.

Embarking on a global quest, we curate the finest shell fabrics and tiniest details, sourced from China, Taiwan, Thailand, Malaysia, India, and beyond, all tailored to your needs.

Our team is built with experienced merchandisers and a hard working QC team, who work to maintain the required AQL and operate cohesively to satisfy our customers’ needs to execute orders of all sizes.

Alongside providing the best products, we also maintain committed business partnerships. We have built a foundation of reliable sourcing of fashion garments from Bangladesh, that too at great value.

Join us in this journey to experience a partnership where professionalism meets comfort!
